Programming Gems

This is a collection of articles recording some of the existing wisdom and practice on how to program well in Lua.
Lua Programming Gems
edited by L. H. de Figueiredo, W. Celes, R. Ierusalimschy
Lua.org, December 2008
ISBN 978-85-903798-4-3 (also available as an e-book)
Read the front matter (containing the preface by the editors, forewords by Cameron Laird and by Mark Hamburg, and short biographies of the contributors) and Chapter 2 on Lua Performance Tips. See also the errata.

Buy a copy of this book and help to support the Lua project.
